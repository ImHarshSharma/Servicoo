# Servicoo

Crafting a fully responsive and dynamic website, our expert team utilizes a powerful tech stack, including HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Mongoose. With a user-friendly approach, visitors can easily get in touch with us through a streamlined form integrated into the website. The data submitted through the form is securely stored in our database. Subsequently, our dedicated team leverages this information to establish direct communication with our valued customers
